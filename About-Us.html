Our Story

At Food lover, we believe in creating unforgettable dining experiences. Founded in 2015, our restaurant blends tradition with innovation — bringing you dishes crafted with locally sourced ingredients, bold flavors, and artistic presentation.

Whether it's a romantic evening or a family celebration, we offer a cozy and elegant atmosphere designed to make every guest feel special.

Meet Our Chef

Chef Arjun Mehta

With over 20 years in the culinary industry, Chef Arjun brings creativity, soul, and precision to every dish. Trained in both Indian and French cuisines, his passion drives the menu and inspires our entire team.
